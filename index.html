Intense Home Ab Workout Routine

Score: 0

This intense two-week ab workout routine is designed to help you achieve a visible six-pack with advanced exercises targeting all areas of your core.

Day 1: Upper Abs Focus

  • Weighted Crunches: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Hanging Leg Raises: 4 sets of 15 reps
  • Toe Touches with Dumbbell: 4 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank with Arm Lift: 4 sets of 60 seconds (30 seconds each arm)

Day 2: Lower Abs Focus

  • Dragon Flags: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Flutter Kicks with Ankle Weights: 4 sets of 30 reps (each leg)
  • Hanging Windshield Wipers: 4 sets of 15 reps (each side)
  • Decline Sit-ups: 4 sets of 15 reps

Day 3: Obliques Focus

  • Weighted Russian Twists: 4 sets of 30 reps (each side)
  • Bicycle Crunches with Resistance Bands: 4 sets of 30 reps (each side)
  • Side Plank with Hip Dips: 4 sets of 20 reps (each side)
  • Oblique V-Ups: 4 sets of 15 reps (each side)

Day 4: Deep Core Focus

  • Weighted Dead Bug: 4 sets of 20 reps (each side)
  • Bird Dog with Dumbbells: 4 sets of 20 reps (each side)
  • Hollow Body Rock: 4 sets of 45 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers with Sliders: 4 sets of 30 reps (each leg)

Day 5: Compound Core Exercises

  • V-Ups with Medicine Ball: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Plank to Push-Up: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Lying Leg Raises with Hip Thrust: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Plank Jacks with Resistance Bands: 4 sets of 30 reps

Day 6: Full Core Routine

  • Crunches with Twist: 4 sets of 30 reps
  • Hanging Leg Raises with Twist: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Russian Twists with Medicine Ball: 4 sets of 30 reps (each side)
  • Plank with Leg Lift: 4 sets of 60 seconds (30 seconds each leg)

Day 7: Active Recovery

Yoga or stretching focusing on gentle core stretches and poses to improve flexibility and aid recovery.

Tips for Success

  • Form Over Reps: Ensure proper form to maximize effectiveness and prevent injury.
  • Engage the Core: Keep your core muscles engaged during each exercise.
  • Breathing: Exhale during exertion and inhale during relaxation.
  • Rest: Take additional rest days if you feel too sore or perform a lighter workout.

Cool-down Stretches

  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 1 minute
  • Child's Pose: 1 minute
  • Cobra Stretch: 1 minute
  • Seated Forward Bend: 1 minute

Stay consistent and focused on your goals!
